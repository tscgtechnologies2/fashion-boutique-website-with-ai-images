Frequently asked

Before your appointment.

Useful details about styling, alterations, gifting and collection updates.

Do you offer styling appointments?+

Yes. Appointments can focus on a single look, a capsule edit, an occasion or a wider wardrobe goal.

Can I request alterations?+

Yes. Minor fit refinements and selected alterations can be discussed during your styling appointment.

Do you have gift options?+

Gift experiences and flexible boutique credit are available for styling, occasions and wardrobe updates.

Are there size consultations?+

Yes. We can help assess proportions, preferred fit and the most suitable silhouettes across the collection.

How often do collections change?+

Core edits evolve throughout the year, with focused seasonal drops and limited signature additions.

Do you offer wardrobe edits?+

Yes. Wardrobe curation identifies what to keep, alter, combine or replace for a clearer overall direction.
